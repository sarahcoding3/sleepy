Curl up with these sites

Reviewed by Sarah C. | May 9th, 2025

Taking a trip? Don't let your sleep lag behind!

This calculator was developed by Jay Olsen PhD. adapted for a website full of helpful calculators for all of our statistic fans. This calculator can help you determine how to shift your sleep so you can be prepared for your out of time zone trip as well as detailed accounts for how to make this move effortless.

Website quality:

Amazing collection of mattress reviews

Forbes Vetted is an amazing subset of a website that provides reviews on everything from makeup to mattresses. Here their team reviews 37 different ones, something that our team at Dreamweaver couldn't do. They have helpful categories, price lists, and ratings for everything someone could want from a mattress.

Website quality:

Something wrong? You might have a sleep disorder!

The American Psychiatric Association has compiled a list of sleep disorders, symptoms, as well as what to do about it. Their website is a bit difficult to navigate with a lot of moving parts, but the information is essentially valuable for you to bring up with your doctor.

Website quality:

In REM? What do your dreams mean?

The Dream Dictionary goes through every possible thing you could have dreamed about and offers you an interpretation on what it could mean. It has pages for good dreams, nightmares, and common symbols that show up. It is beautifully designed and organized.

Website quality:

White noise maker

For those that can't sleep, try listening to white noise or ASMR! This website has a great variety of noise at different Hz to help you focus, relax, and be informed on what it can do. There is so much information and a high learning curve, but if you figure it out, it can be a great benefit to your sleep.
