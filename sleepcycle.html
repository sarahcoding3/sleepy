What is the sleep cycle?

Legend:

: Sleep Stage

: Symptom

The first stage of sleep

This is when you first fall asleep. Your body and brain start to slow down but your muscles won't fully relax yet.You are pretty easy to wake up during this stage.

: 1 - 7 minutes

Deepness rating:



The second stage of sleep

You spend the most time of your sleep cycle in this stage. Your brain resists being woken up by showing a new brainwave pattern and giving off short bursts of activity.

: 10 - 25 minutes

Deepness rating:



The third stage of sleep

Your brain activity starts making delta waves!This part of your sleep cycle is essential for consolidating memory, increasing creativity, as well as helping your body grow and recover from the day. You spend the most time in this stage in the earlier parts of the night, but as your sleep cycle repeats, you may spend more time in REM.

: 20 - 40 minutes

Deepness rating:



The last stage of sleep

Your brain activity starts making delta waves!This part of your sleep cycle is essential for consolidating memory, increasing creativity, as well as helping your body grow and recover from the day. You spend the most time in this stage in the earlier parts of the night, but as your sleep cycle repeats, you may spend more time in REM.

: 2 - 60 minutes
